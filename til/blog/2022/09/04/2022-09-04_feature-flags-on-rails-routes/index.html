Feature Flags On Rails Routes

rails flipper

Found a tweet explaining how to wrap a rails route with a feature flag.

resources :media,
      constraints: ->(req) do
                     current_user = req.env["warden"].user(:user)
                     Flipper.enabled?(:media_uploads, current_user)
                   end
